KUANY ANDREA ANEI


Kuany was born 1996 in a small village called Dooc Agany Makeer. A village that was later renamed after Agany Makeer's son Anei Akeen Agany, Kuany's father. Kuany is from Pakueny clan of Abuok Marol, a sub-community of Apuk Giir Thiik, Dinka tribe.Located South West of the county's Capital Lietnhom, South of the Payam's Capital Ajogo and North of his Boma Mayom Chol. Dooc is a small village in Gogrial East County (Apuk Giir Thiik). Kuany was born to his mother Akuch Madut Akuei from Apol, another sub-community of Apuk Giir Thiik.

EARLY LIFE AND SIBBLINGS

Born with his other six sibblins, Kuany was the last born. Jongkor the first born who was happily married her husband Hol Akoon, sadly passed in 2010 leaving her kids behind. Akot, Anei, Athiang, Ajak, Aliel and Ayak all were eventually were left behind. Being the last born, Kuany, was afforded some love and care not only by his parents but also from his elder sibbings especially Agany who later took care of him all the from his teenage age. Kuany was lucky to have experiences of both rural and urban with his infant age being dominated by village experiences where he used to look after goats and cows before permantly moving to live in urban. He was a very good boy that was regarded a leader by the elders.

EDUCATIONAL BACKGROUND

Kuany began his Primary education in his Boma Mayom Chol in 2008 before moving to Juba and eventually rounding off his Primary studies in Kampala Uganda in a school called Queen of Peace. Kuany would go ahead to join High School in 2015 in Goodwill College before again moving back to Juba to do his final year (Senior Four) in Juba Academy Secondary School. Having not had interest in going to school in his early life, Kuany began his Primary School at the age of 13 however, Kuany was a geniues who with skipped classes, holday studies, managed to sit for his Primary Leaving Examinations in 2014 at the age of 18 with eight aggregates, a Supper First Grade. After finishing his Highschool in Juba Academy in Academy Year 2017-18, Kuany began his university in 2020 at the University of Juba Kuany undertook a career in Computer Science and Information Technology, specializzing in Information Technology. A career that took him five years to accomplish (Honors Degreee) from 2020-25. Kuany had a strong grib over Programming and web development using Programming Language like JAVA, PYTHON, JAVASCRIPT and HTML plus CSS. Having picked some interest in computer, Kuany went ahead to do multiple courses in computer in Kampala which made him a standout among his classmates.
